---
title: "Five papers (two spotlights) accepted to ICML 2026"
date: 2026-04-30
---

- Congratulations to the authors! The following papers were accepted to ICML 2026.
- Chunsan Hong, Sanghyun Lee, Jong Chul Ye, *Unifying Masked Diffusion Models with Various Generation Orders and Beyond* (spotlight, top 2.2%).
- Jaemin Kim et al., *Universal Reasoner: A Single, Composable Plug-and-Play Reasoner for Frozen LLMs*.
- Jaa-Yeon Lee, Yeobin Hong, Taesung Kwon, Jong Chul Ye, *Alignment-Guided Score Matching for Text-to-Image Alignment* (spotlight, top 2.2%).
- Beomsu Kim, ByungHee Cha, Jong Chul Ye, *Align Your Trajectory Tangent: Training Better Consistency Models via Manifold-Aligned Tangents*.
- Jinho Chang et al., *ContrastiveCFG: Guiding Diffusion Sampling by Contrasting Positive and Negative Concepts*.
