About Us

Application-inspired fundamental research

Our research activities are primarily focused on signal processing and machine learning for high-resolution, high-sensitivity image reconstruction from real-world biomedical imaging systems. Such problems lead to investigations of fundamental problems across physics, mathematics, signal processing, biology, and medicine. We believe that actual imaging applications are a source of endless inspiration for new mathematical theories — and we are eager to solve both specific applications and application-inspired fundamental theoretical problems.
