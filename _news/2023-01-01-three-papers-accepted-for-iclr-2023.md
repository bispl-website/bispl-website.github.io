- Boah Kim, Yujin Oh, Jong Chul Ye,"Diffusion Adversarial Representation Learning for Self-supervised Vessel Segmentation", International Conference on Learning Representations (ICLR ), 2023
- Gihyun Kwon, Jong Chul Ye, "Diffusion-based Image Translation using disentangled style and content representation", International Conference on Learning Representations (ICLR ), 2023
