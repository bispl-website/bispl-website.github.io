Congratulations! The following four papers are accepted for CVPR 2024.
- Hyelin Nam, Gihyun Kwon, Geon Yeong Park, Jong Chul Ye,"Contrastive Denoising Score for Text-guided Latent Diffusion Image Editing", in the Proceedings of The IEEE/CVF Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR), 2024.
- Hyeonho Jeong, Geon Yeong Park, Jong Chul Ye, "One-Shot Video Motion Customization using Temporal Attention Adaption for Text-to-Video Diffusion Models", in the Proceedings of The IEEE/CVF Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR), 2024.
- Geon Yeong Park, Chanyong Jung, Sangmin Lee, Jong Chul Ye, Sang Wan Lee,"Self-supervised debiasing using low rank regularization", in the Proceedings of The IEEE/CVF Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR), 2024.
- Gihyun Kwon, Simon Jenni, Dingzeyu Li, Joon-Young Lee, Jong Chul Ye, Fabian Caba Heilbron, "Concept Weaver: Enabling Multi-Concept Fusion in Text-to-Image Models", in the Proceedings of The IEEE/CVF Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR), 2024.
