We got the 3rd place award at CVPR NTIRE (New Trends in Image Restoration and Enhancement workshop) on Super-Resolution Imaging Challenge !! [http://www.vision.ee.ethz.ch/ntire17/](http://www.vision.ee.ethz.ch/ntire17/)

Our team (lab402) won the 3rd place award at the CVPR NTIRE super-resolution image challenge. The related workshop paper can be found from the following link: Woong Bae, Jaejun Yoo, Jong Chul Ye, Beyond Deep Residual Learning for Image Restoration: Persistent Homology-Guided Manifold Simplification, *The IEEE Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR) Workshops*, 2017, pp. 145-153
