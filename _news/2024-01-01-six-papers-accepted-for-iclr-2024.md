Congratulations! The following six papers from BISPL have been accepted for presentation at the 2024 International Conference on Learning Representations (ICLR).
- Hyeonho Jeong, Jong Chul Ye,"Ground-A-Video: Zero-shot Grounded Video Editing using Text-to-image Diffusion Models", International Conference on Learning Representations (ICLR ), 2024.
- Hyungjin Chung, Suhyeon Lee, Jong Chul Ye,, "Decomposed Diffusion Sampler for Accelerating Large-Scale Inverse Problems", International Conference on Learning Representations (ICLR ), 2024.
- Beomsu Kim, Gihyun Kwon, Kwanyoung Kim, Jong Chul Ye,"Unpaired Image-to-Image Translation via Neural Schrödinger Bridge", International Conference on Learning Representations (ICLR ), 2024.
- Soobin Um, Suhyeon Lee, Jong Chul Ye,"Don't Play Favorites: Minority Guidance for Diffusion Models", International Conference on Learning Representations (ICLR ), 2024.
- JangHo Park, Gihyun Kwon, Jong Chul Ye,"ED-NeRF: Efficient Text-Guided Editing of 3D Scene With Latent Space NeRF", International Conference on Learning Representations (ICLR ), 2024.
- Suhyeon Lee, Won Jun Kim, Jinho Chang, Jong Chul Ye,"LLM-CXR: Instruction-Finetuned LLM for CXR Image Understanding and Generation", International Conference on Learning Representations (ICLR ), 2024.
