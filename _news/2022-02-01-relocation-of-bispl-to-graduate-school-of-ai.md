BISPL has joined the[Graduate School of AI](https://gsai.kaist.ac.kr/) as of January 3rd, 2022 in order to focus more on the machine learning and artificial intelligence for healthcare. Accordingly, we are accepting students only through the Graduate School of AI. For students who are interested in joining BISPL, **please apply to the Graduate School of AI (** https://gsai.kaist.ac.kr/admission/) or contact Prof. Jong Chul Ye.
