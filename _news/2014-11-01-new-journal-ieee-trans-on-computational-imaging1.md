A new journal IEEE Trans. on Comptuational Imaging (TCI) is now accepting papers. The IEEE Transactions on Computational Imaging will publish articles that will cover all areas of computational imaging ranging from fundamental theoretical methods to the latest innovative computational imaging system designs.

I had been nominated by IEEE EMBS Technical Committee for Biomedical Imaging and Image Processing (BIIP) and became one of the **Technical Liasion Committee** of the journal. Thanks BIIP colleagues for the nominations ! In addition, I started to serve as the **Associate Editor** for the journal TCI. It is really a great honor for me to become the inaugural AE and steering committee !! Now, we started to see the submissions to TCI and I myself recently handled one of the papers.

I am also happy to witness that the imaging has become one of the most fast growing areas of research and the computational imaging plays the important role to make it happen. The introduction of TCI now clearly reflect the important of the computational imaging.
