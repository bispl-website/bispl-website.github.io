BISPL member Kyoung Hwan Jin has been recently awarded the Silver Medal Award from Samsung Human Tech Thesis Award in Signal Processing Area. The work is on the image interpolation method from irregular sampled data. In this paper, Kyoung Hwan made a very intelligent low rank matrix completion method. The paper is currently in revision by IEEE Trans. Image Processing. Congratulations !
