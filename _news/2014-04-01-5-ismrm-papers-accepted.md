We have quite a significant presence at upcoming ISMRM, OHBM, and ISBI. The following is the list of the papers we are going to present at the upcoming conferences.
**ISMRM 2014**
- "Accelerated In Vivo 3D Chemical Exchange Saturation Transfer (CEST) Imaging using dynamic Compressed Sensing," by Juyoung Lee, et al.
- "Region adaptive motion compensated dynamic CS for cardiac perfusion imaging", by Heesui Yoon, et al
- "Resolution improvement of 3D DCE-MRI using dynamic CS with patch-based non-convex low rank penalty" by Heesui Yoon, et al
- "Accelerated T2 Prime Mapping using Dynamic Compressed Sensing with Patch-based Low-Rank Penalty", by Dongwook Lee, et al
- "Dynamic Simultaneous T1 and T2* weighted 3D Dual-Echo Imaging with Compressed Sensing: Potential Advancement to Simultaneous DCE and DSC Imaging", by Dongwook Lee et al

**OHBM 2014**
- "functional connectivity studies of the default mode network in Parkinson's disease with cognitive impairment", Maryam Ghahremani, et al
- "Topological Analysis of EEG Connectivity Patterns of Depressed Patients using Persistence Landscape", Jaejun Yoo, et al

**ISBI 2014**
- "ECG-gated Cardiac CT reconstruction using Patch based Low Rank Regularization", by Kyungsang Kim, et al
- "TOF-PET ordered subset reconstruction using non-uniform separable quadratic surrogates algorithm" by Kyungsang Kim, et al
- "Accurate inversion of absorption and scattering in diffuse optical tomography without iterative Green’s function update" by Okkyun Lee, et al
- "T2 prime mapping from highly undersampled data using compressed sensing with patch based low rank penalty", by Dongwook Lee, et al

Congratulations, Team BISPL !
