Prof. Ye is selected as a Fellow of Korean Academy of Science and Technology (KAST). The KAIST Fellows are selected through a three-stage evaluation process for individuals who have been active in the field of science and technology for more than 20 years, and have made significant contributions to the advancement of their respective fields with innovative research achievements.
