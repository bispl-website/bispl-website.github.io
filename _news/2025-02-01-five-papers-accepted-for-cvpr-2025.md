- Hyelin Nam, Jaemin Kim, Dohun Lee, Jong Chul Ye,"[Optical-Flow Guided Prompt Optimization for Coherent Video Generation](https://openreview.net/forum?id=D6KS8EwmSF&referrer=%5BAuthor%20Console%5D(%2Fgroup%3Fid%3Dthecvf.com%2FCVPR%2F2025%2FConference%2FAuthors%23your-submissions))", IEEE/CVF Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR ), 2025.
- Won Jun Kim, Hyungjin Chung, Jaemin Kim, Sangmin Lee, Byeongsu Sim, Jong Chul Ye,"[Derivative-Free Diffusion Manifold-Constrained Gradient for Unified XAI](https://openreview.net/forum?id=zwla7O3Hsv&referrer=%5BAuthor%20Console%5D(%2Fgroup%3Fid%3Dthecvf.com%2FCVPR%2F2025%2FConference%2FAuthors%23your-submissions))", IEEE/CVF Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR ), 2025.

- Hyeonho Jeong, Chun-Hao Paul Huang, Jong Chul Ye, Niloy Mitra, Duygu Ceylan, "[Track4Gen: Teaching Video Diffusion Models to Track Points Improves Video Generation](https://openreview.net/forum?id=nhVEAs057C&referrer=%5BAuthor%20Console%5D(%2Fgroup%3Fid%3Dthecvf.com%2FCVPR%2F2025%2FConference%2FAuthors%23your-submissions))", IEEE/CVF Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR ), 2025.
